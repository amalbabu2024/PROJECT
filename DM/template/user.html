Safety Tips

It's essential to stay prepared for disasters. Here are some general safety tips:

  • Have an emergency kit ready with essential supplies.
  • Stay informed through local authorities and news outlets.
  • Follow evacuation orders and seek higher ground in floods.
  • Keep a communication plan with your family.
